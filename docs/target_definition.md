# Target definition

`Tmax_d` is the maximum observed 2 m air temperature during local day `d`, from `00:00:00` to `23:59:59` in `Europe/Berlin`.

Forecast probabilities are integer Celsius bins:

`Tmax = 22C` means `21.5 <= actual Tmax < 22.5`.

Primary truth: DWD 10-minute air temperature for station `01262` `Muenchen-Flughafen`.
